# 100 LINQ Practice Exercises

Sharpen your C# skills with **100 carefully designed LINQ exercises** covering real-world scenarios. This repository is perfect for developers who want to **master LINQ queries**, improve **data manipulation skills**, and prepare for **coding interviews**.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Comprehensive Coverage:** Exercises range from **basic operations** to **complex filtering, grouping, and aggregations**.  
- **Practical Scenarios:** Work with **products, customers, orders, employees, projects, and library data**.  
- **Optimized LINQ Queries:** Learn to write **clean, efficient, and readable** LINQ expressions.  
- **Hands-On Practice:** Includes **sample data generation** for testing all exercises easily.  

---

## 📂 Structure

- **Basic Operations & Aggregations (Q1–Q10)** – Sum, average, count, top N items, distinct values.  
- **Filtering & Joins (Q11–Q20)** – Multi-collection queries and relationship-based filtering.  
- **Advanced Grouping & Calculations (Q21–Q30)** – Group data, calculate totals, averages, and percentages.  
- **Complex Filtering & Comparisons (Q31–Q40)** – Multi-condition queries and advanced comparisons.  
- **Set Operations & Advanced Queries (Q41–Q50)** – Union, intersection, lookups, and ranking.  
- **Orders & Customers (Q51–Q70)** – Aggregate, filter, and analyze nested collection data.  
- **Books & Library (Q71–Q80)** – Borrowing patterns, genre analysis, and late returns.  
- **Employees & Departments (Q81–Q90)** – Salary and experience analysis, promotions, and reporting.  
- **Projects & Assignments (Q91–Q100)** – Duration, budgets, multi-employee participation, and project performance.
